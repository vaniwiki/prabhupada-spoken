[Prabhupāda converses in Bengali with guests for a couple of minutes]

**Prabhupāda:**
Where is Kṣīrodakaśāyī? Hmm? So that... You want to go immediately?

**Kṣīrodakaśāyī:**
Quarter to eleven I give the time to start from here to reach...

**Prabhupāda:**
What is the time now?

**Kṣīrodakaśāyī:**
Twenty past...

**Devotee:**
Ten fifteen.

**Kṣīrodakaśāyī:**
Quarter past ten.

**Prabhupāda:**
So you want the check immediately?

**Kṣīrodakaśāyī:**
We have to buy the stamp, Prabhupāda.

**Prabhupāda:**
No, the check will not buy stamp; you have to take money.

**Kṣīrodakaśāyī:**
Yes, I will give the check to the man, and then he will give the cash to..., before we go to Mathurā.

[Prabhupāda converses in Bengali with guests] [break] [end]
