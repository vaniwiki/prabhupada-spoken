**Prabhupāda:**
So your spiritual name is Abhirāma...?

**Karandhara:**
Manobhirāma.

**Prabhupāda:**
Manobhirāma. Manobhirāma. Manobhirāma is Kṛṣṇa. He pleases everyone's mind.

**Devotees:**
//Jaya. Haribol.//

**Karandhara:**
David? [break]

**Prabhupāda:**
There is all neck beads, everyone? You knows the rules and regulation? [break] Dhanurdharī. [aside:] Not left. Yes. Dhanurdharī. Dhanurdharī is Lord Rāmacandra's name. [pause]

**Karandhara:**
Gilbert? [to Prabhupāda:] Gambhīra.

**Prabhupāda:**
Gambhīra. You know the rules and regulation? Here, take. Your spiritual name, Gambhīra dāsa, "very grave." That is Kṛṣṇa.

**Karandhara:**
Bill?

**Prabhupāda:**
[//japa//] [break] Bāṅke-bihārī.

**Devotees:**
Oh, //haribol.//

**Prabhupāda:**
//Bāṅke// means "curved." Kṛṣṇa stands always this. That is Bāṅke-bihārī.

**Karandhara:**
Hannah?

**Prabhupāda:**
He is not very straight, Kṛṣṇa. People are crooked, so He is the most crooked. [break]

**Karandhara:**
Hari-sevakā.

**Prabhupāda:**
Hari-sevikā. So, Hari-sevikā, Hari-sevikā, "maidservant of Kṛṣṇa."

**Devotees:**
//Jaya.// [break]

**Karandhara:**
Delores? [break]

**Prabhupāda:**
Greedy for Kṛṣṇa. //Tatra laulyam eka laulyam.// To get Kṛṣṇa, you have only one price: to become greedy for Kṛṣṇa.

**Devotees:**
//Jaya!//

**Prabhupāda:**
That's all. That is the only... [break]... //Tatra laulyam ekalaṁ mūlyaṁ na janma-koṭibhiḥ sukṛtibhir labhyate// [[books:cc:madhya:8:70|[Cc. Madhya 8.70]]]. [break]

**Karandhara:**
Vidya.

**Prabhupāda:**
Divya. Divya. Your spiritual name is Divya. Divya means "spiritual." [pause]

**Karandhara:**
Dhīra-sevā.

**Prabhupāda:**
Huh?

**Karandhara:**
Dhīra-sevā.

**Prabhupāda:**
Dhīra. Dhīra-sevā. Dhīra-lalitā. Dhīra-sevā? No. Dhīra-sevī. She is woman. [to devotee:] You know all the rules and regulation? Yes. Your name is Dhīra-sevī. Kṛṣṇa is Dhīra. Dhīra-lalitā.

**Karandhara:**
Sajjana-jīvana.

**Prabhupāda:**
Sajjana-jīvana. You know rules and regulation?

**Devotee:**
No meat-eating, no [indistinct].

**Prabhupāda:**
Yes. Sajjana-jīvana. Sajjana-jīvana. Life is very gentle.

So thank you very much. [devotees exclaim]

Now begin chanting and //yajña. Yajñaiḥ saṅkīrtanaiḥ prāyair yajanti hi su-medhasaḥ// [SB 11.5.32]. That is the //yajña, saṅkīrtana.//

**Devotee:**
Hare Kṛṣṇa [end]
