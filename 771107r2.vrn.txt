**Kavirāja:**
[Hindi with Prabhupāda]

[background conversation about bathing Prabhupāda]

**Tamāla Kṛṣṇa:**
We do it about two times a day with a towel.

**Kavirāja:**
[Hindi with Bhakti-caru]

**Tamāla Kṛṣṇa:**
Rose water? Water? [indistinct comments by Upendra]

**Kavirāja:**
[Hindi with Prabhupāda; asks about //parikramā//]

**Tamāla Kṛṣṇa:**
You have to give Prabhupāda some strength to go on the //parikramā.//

**Svarūpa Dāmodara:**
He said in three days.

**Tamāla Kṛṣṇa:**
Three days. That's good.

**Kavirāja:**
[Hindi with Bhakti-caru and Prabhupāda about different foods]

**Bhakti-caru:**
[Hindi about //cāpāṭi// and //ḍāl//]

**Tamāla Kṛṣṇa:**
But whether Prabhupāda will eat some? Prabhupāda, you will try to eat the Gujarati //roṭī// and //ḍāl?//

**Prabhupāda:**
Hmm.

**Kavirāja:**
[Hindi]

**Prabhupāda:**
[Hindi]

**Bhakti-caru:**
[long Hindi conversation about different foods and taste with Prabhupāda and //kavirāja//] Śrīla Prabhupāda? [Hindi] [break]

**Tamāla Kṛṣṇa:**
...Gujarati //roṭīs?//

**Bhakti-caru:**
[Hindi about Gujarati //roṭīs// and //ḍāl// and rice, //khicuṛi, purīs,// chutney]

**Prabhupāda:**
[Hindi with Bhakti-caru] //Mālpura// I'll take.

**Tamāla Kṛṣṇa:**
//Mālpura.//

**Bhakti-caru:**
[Hindi]

**Tamāla Kṛṣṇa:**
You want to try some //mālpura// later on tonight?

**Bhakti-caru:**
[Hindi with Prabhupāda] Prabhupāda will let us know. [Hindi] He says one too hot. Just like the way it was today. [Hindi with Prabhupāda; suggests //śrīkand//]

**Tamāla Kṛṣṇa:**
//Śrīkand.// That's very good for you, Śrīla Prabhupāda, //śrīkand.//

**Prabhupāda:**
Oh, //śrīkand.//

**Tamāla Kṛṣṇa:**
That's a good preparation for you, Śrīla Prabhupāda, very nutritious.

**Bhakti-caru:**
[Hindi with Prabhupāda about different foods]

**Prabhupāda:**
So, where is Baradrāj?

**Tamāla Kṛṣṇa:**
Baradrāj is here, Śrīla Prabhupāda.

**Prabhupāda:**
So you can sing.

**Tamāla Kṛṣṇa:**
//Jaya// Śrīla Prabhupāda.

**Prabhupāda:**
Softly.

**Baradrāj:**
Softly. [break] [end]
