====== Room Conversation ======

**Prabhupāda:**
We are discussing some subject matter from //Reader's Digest// about the next two hundred years, how things will happen. The scientist proposing that they'll control the laws of nature, and they will produce food from garbage---so many fantastic ideas. But our suggestion, according to Caitanya Mahāprabhu,

> harer nāma harer nāma harer nāma iva kevalam
> kalau nāsty eva nāsty eva nāsty eva gatir anyathā
> [[books:cc:adi:17:21|[Cc. Ādi 17.21]]]

[end]
