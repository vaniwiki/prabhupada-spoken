====== Room Conversation with Jaidayal Dalmia ======

**Prabhupāda:**
//Jaya.// [Hindi]

**Jaidayal Dalmia:**
[Hindi]

**Prabhupāda:**
[Hindi about the //Gītā// conference with guest] There is good demand all over the world. [Hindi] "Kṛṣṇa is not person; Kṛṣṇa is consciousness." [Hindi] Kṛṣṇa personally speaking, and "He is consciousness." He says, //yadā yadā hi dharmasya glānir bhavati bhārata// [[books:bg:4:7|[Bg. 4.7]]]: "I come down." [Hindi] Māyāvādī. Caitanya Mahāprabhu has condemned, //māyāvādi-bhāṣya śunile haya sarva-nāśa// [[books:cc:madhya:6:169|[Cc. Madhya 6.169]]]. //Caitanya-caritāmṛta he.// [Hindi] //Yogaḥ proktaḥ purātanaḥ// [[books:bg:4:3|[Bg. 4.3]]]. Kṛṣṇa said that "I am speaking to you the old //yoga// system." [Hindi] ...not my interpretation. [Hindi] [break] Kṛṣṇa is person. Where is that verse in the Tenth Canto?

**Tamāla Kṛṣṇa:**
In the //Bhagavad-gītā?//

**Prabhupāda:**
//Paraṁ brahma paraṁ dhāma pavitraṁ paramaṁ bhavān// [[books:bg:10:12-13|[Bg. 10.12]]].

**Jayādvaita:**
Tenth Chapter.

**Tamāla Kṛṣṇa:**

> paraṁ brahma paraṁ dhāma
> pavitraṁ paramaṁ bhavān
> puruṣaṁ śāśvataṁ divyam
> ādi-devam ajaṁ vibhum
> [[books:bg:10:12-13|[Bg. 10.12]]]

**Prabhupāda:**
//Puruṣaṁ śāśvatam. Govindam ādi-puruṣam.// [break] This is understanding of //Bhagavad-gītā.// But they'll not do that. What can be done? They cannot preach //Bhagavad-gītā. Sarvam etad ṛtaṁ manye// [[books:bg:10:14|[Bg. 10.14]]]. Next line?

**Tamāla Kṛṣṇa:**
//Yan māṁ vadasi keśava, na hi te bhagavan vyaktiṁ vidur devā na dānavāḥ.//

**Prabhupāda:**
Ah. [Hindi] "I have come personally." //Yadā yadā hi dharmasya// [[books:bg:4:7|[Bg. 4.7]]]. //Bhagavad-gītā.// [Hindi] ...as it is. [Hindi] //Bhagavad-gītā// as it is, never say it. [Hindi] ...imagination... [Hindi] Simply waste of time. //Śrama eva hi kevalam// [[books:sb:1:2:8|[SB 1.2.8]]]. //Vāsudeve bhagavati bhakti-yogaḥ prayojitaḥ, janayaty āśu vairāgyam// [[books:sb:1:2:7|[SB 1.2.7]]]. //Janayaty āśu vairāgyam.// [Hindi] //Yaḥ śāstra-vidhim utsṛjya vartate kāma-kārataḥ, na siddhiṁ sāvāpnoti// [[books:bg:16:23|[Bg. 16.23]]]. [Hindi] //Śrama eva hi kevalam.// [Hindi with guest] There is good demand all over the world. [Hindi about American devotees] Nobody can turn them now. //Jaya.// Thank you very much.

**Bhakti-caru:**
Śrīla Prabhupāda? [Bengali] [end]
